NF DOMESTIKIT

A repository for Domestikit as a housing-service research environment, developed from Cedric Price, Buckminster Fuller, postwar prefabrication, and related work on anticipatory dwelling, short-life housing, and domestic systems.

The page is intentionally minimal: an index, a navigation surface, and a stable frame for future entries, drawings, facsimiles, timelines, and catalogue objects.

Timelines

A compact chronology to anchor the repository.

1944–1949
Temporary Housing Programme, factory-made prefabs, and the public testing of industrial housing models.
1965–1971
Cedric Price’s steel-house studies, 24 Hour Living Toy, and the Short Life Housing Study.
1972–1977
Domestikit begins as a working proposition and acquires its catalogue form and research identity.
Present
Repository building, retrospective interpretation, and the preparation of follow-up articles under Norman Fellows.
